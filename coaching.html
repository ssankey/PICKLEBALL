This is NOT a 1-Size-Fits-All Methodology

If you take 10 of the top pickleball players in the country, you’ll see that they each have different styles, techniques and form, depending on their racket sport background (or lack thereof).
Prem could teach you how HE hits the ball, but he has spent hundreds of hours practicing pickleball, table tennis, cricket and other sports at the national level, so there is no way you could have the exact same shot that HE does. Nor should you!
At The Pickleball Guru Academy, I look at YOUR game. I assess and analyze YOUR strengths, limitations, athletic ability, background and help YOU play Smart Pickleball.
